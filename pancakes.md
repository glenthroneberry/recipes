## Instructions

1. Whisk flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt together.
2. In a separate bowl, whisk milk, egg, and melted butter.
3. Combine wet and dry ingredients, stirring just until no dry streaks remain (a few lumps are fine).
4. Pour 1/4 cup batter per pancake onto a hot, greased griddle.
5. Cook until bubbles form on top, then flip and cook until golden.

## Notes

Batter can rest in the fridge overnight for a lighter texture.
