## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a loaf pan.
2. In a bowl, mix mashed bananas with melted butter.
3. Stir in sugar, egg, and vanilla.
4. Sprinkle baking soda and salt over the mixture, then stir in.
5. Add flour and mix until just combined.
6. Pour into the loaf pan and bake for 60 minutes, until a toothpick comes out clean.

## Notes

Add a handful of walnuts or chocolate chips if you like.
