## Instructions

1. Dice onion, steak, and potato
2. Preheat pan to medium heat
3. Add steak and onion to pan
4. Season with salt, pepper, garlic
5. Brown steak and onion
6. Once mostly cooked through, add potatoes
7. Reduce heat to med-low and cover
8. Allow potatoes to get soft
9. Heat up tortillas
10. Add burrito mix to tortilla with cheese and salsa
